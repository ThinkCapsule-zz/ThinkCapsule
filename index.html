About Us

ThinkCapsule is a company that strives to bring creative ideas to life. We are founded on the principle that there are many answers to problems, though few are done the right way. We believe in providing endless opportunities and possibilities for the younger generation to try new things. We are risk takers who combine insight and calculated decisions in every step we take. We are led by a management team who comes from passionate and diverse backgrounds of Analytics, Design and Science.
